Our Company Story

Wehanda was founded with a mission to empower local businesses by providing a commission-free platform, enabling them to thrive without the burden of exorbitant fees imposed by major food delivery services like Uber Eats. Our goal is to create a supportive environment where local entrepreneurs can boost their business and retain more of their hard-earned profits.

What We Offer

Simple Click-to-Order Experience

Enable customers to conveniently place orders for pickup or delivery directly through our platform. Integrate our order platform with Google Maps, Yelp, and more to provide direct order links, saving you commission fees from platforms like UberEats, DoorDash, and others.

Advanced Order Handling

Keep both business owners and customers informed with real-time notifications about order statuses. Utilize our bump screen for smooth order management. Benefit from flexible payment options by using our integrated payment processing system or continue with your preferred card payment processor like Stripe, without additional fees.

Easy! Yet Robust Online Presence

Create a customized online storefront to effectively showcase your menu and services, significantly enhancing your restaurant's online presence.

Centralized Sales Insight

Gain access to detailed sales reports that provide valuable insights into your revenue, enabling you to make informed business decisions.

QR Menu/Dine-in QR Order

Elevate your restaurant's service with QR code menus and QR code ordering directly from the table, streamlining the dining experience for your customers.

Booking System

Effortlessly manage table reservations to ensure a smooth dining experience for your customers. Receive real-time notifications for both business owners and customers to keep track of reservations efficiently.
